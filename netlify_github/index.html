Volunteer Opportunities

Here at KICKZZZ we are always looking for volunteers to model for our products. We can provide you an outlet to start yourself out in the modeling business or even compensate you depending how you want to work for us.

We have a couple of jobs you can apply for in our schedule tab. The main job would be the shoe model. You would come in and take photos modeling the shoes to put on our website (See the carousel above) or modeling the clothing we sell in the store

Please contact our store via email or phone call to setup an appointment or apply online using the Volunteer form under the Schedule tab
